MPG Calculator

MPG Calculator lets you calculate either Litres/100kms or Miles/gallon in either US or UK gallons. Simply enter in your odometer reading before and after your journey and how many litres or gallons of fuel used.

MPG Calculator also lets you enter in an amount in one unit and then it will convert that amount for the other two units so you don't have to.
